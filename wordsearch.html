How to Play

Words from the theme are hidden in the grid — you won't know what they are until you find them.

Drag across letters in any direction (including diagonals and backwards) to select a word.

When you find one it lights up and gets revealed.

Stuck? Tap Reveal Words to see what you're looking for.
