Horizon Zero Dawn Word Search

The Horizon Zero Dawn Word Search hides words from across the game in a 10×10 grid. Drag across the letters to discover hidden words — characters, locations, and key terms from Horizon Zero Dawn are all in there. Words can run in any direction including diagonals and backwards. You won't see the word list upfront — finding each word is part of the challenge. Multiple grids work through the theme from familiar names to more specific terms.
