How I Met Your Mother Word Search

The How I Met Your Mother Word Search hides words from across the series in a 10×10 grid. Drag across the letters to discover hidden words — characters, locations, and key terms from How I Met Your Mother are all in there. Words can run in any direction including diagonals and backwards. You won't see the word list upfront — finding each word is part of the challenge. Multiple grids work through the theme from familiar names to more specific terms.
