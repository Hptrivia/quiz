Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice Wordle

The Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice Wordle pulls words from across the game and challenges you to guess each one a letter at a time. Words span characters, locations, and key terms that define Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice. Green tiles mean the letter is in the right position, yellow means it appears somewhere else in the word, and grey means it is not in the word at all. Each page covers a new set of words, working through the theme from familiar names to more specific terms.
