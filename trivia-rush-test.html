Trivia Rush

Answer fast, build streaks, chase the rush

Questions go Easy → Medium → Hard → Expert. Tap an answer before the 20-second timer runs out.

Easy 100 pts
Medium 200 pts
Hard 300 pts
Expert 400 pts

Every 3 correct answers in a row raises your multiplier by +1x. Every 2 wrong in a row drops it by −1x and hits you with a double deduction. Streaks reset each other.

Wrong answer or timeout deducts points. The higher your score, the bigger the penalty — deductions scale up at 500, 2000, and 4000 points. Score hits 0 and the run ends.

Fill the Heat Meter with 10 consecutive correct answers to unlock Double Points for 3 questions.
