Yellowjackets trivia works because the series splits between a high-school soccer team surviving a wilderness plane crash and the scarred adults they become. Fans can be tested on both timelines, the survivors and the show's dark mysteries.

A strong Yellowjackets quiz can include the soccer team and the crash that strands them, the teen survivors and their adult counterparts like Shauna, Taissa, Natalie and Misty, the wilderness and its rituals, the mystery of what happened over the winter, the relationships and rivalries, and the present-day secrets the survivors are hiding. It works because it rewards fans who track the story across two timelines.
