Arrested Development trivia works because it is a densely layered comedy packed with running jokes, callbacks and absurd family dynamics that reward close attention. Fans can be tested on the Bluth family, the recurring gags, and the show's intricate plotting.

A strong Arrested Development quiz can include Michael Bluth holding the family together, George Michael and Maeby, Gob and his illusions and the Final Countdown, Lindsay and Tobias Funke, Buster and Lucille, George Sr. and Oscar, the banana stand and the model home, the Cornballer, the chicken dances, the never-nudes, narrator gags, and the running jokes and callbacks that define the series.
