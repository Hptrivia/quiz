  • What was Carwood Lipton's postwar career? Glass-making executive
  • What type of artillery pieces did Easy Company actually find at Brecourt, rather than 88s? 105s
  • How many British soldiers are hiding north of the Rhine waiting to be rescued in episode 5? 140
  • Which platoon does Lieutenant Welsh command in Episode 3? 1st Platoon
  • Where was Bill Guarnere's brother killed? Monte Cassino
  • Which unit's medical company was captured by the Germans, leaving Easy Company without surgical support at Bastogne? 326th Medical
  • Which armored unit does George Rice belong to at Bastogne? 10th Armor
  • What country is the nurse Anna originally from mentioned in episode 6? Congo
  • What serial number belonged to Shifty Powers? 13066266
  • What is the term Doc Roe uses for Cajun faith healers like his grandmother? Traiteurs

Band of Brothers trivia works because the miniseries follows the real men of Easy Company from Currahee through D-Day, Bastogne and the fall of Germany. Fans can be tested on the soldiers, the battles and the moments that defined the 101st Airborne.

A strong Band of Brothers quiz can include Easy Company's officers and men from Winters and Nixon to Guarnere, Malarkey and Roe, the training at Camp Toccoa and the run up Currahee, the jumps into Normandy and Holland, the siege of Bastogne, the discovery of the concentration camp, and the capture of the Eagle's Nest. It works because it rewards fans who followed each soldier's story across the campaign.
