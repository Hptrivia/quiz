This Is Us trivia works because it weaves together past and present to tell an emotional, interconnected story about one family across generations. Fans can be tested on the Pearsons, the timelines, and the show's heartbreaking twists.

A strong This Is Us quiz can include Jack and Rebecca Pearson, the Big Three Kevin, Kate and Randall, Beth, Toby, Miguel and Nicky, Randall's adoption and his birth father William, Kevin's acting career, Kate's journey, the multiple timelines and flashbacks, the crockpot and the cabin, the major reveals and losses, and the themes of family, memory and love that define the series.
