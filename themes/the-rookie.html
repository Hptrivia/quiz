The Rookie trivia works because it mixes procedural police work with personal drama and humour, following an older recruit proving himself on the job. Fans can be tested on John Nolan, the officers of Mid-Wilshire, and the show's cases.

A strong The Rookie quiz can include John Nolan and his late-career start, his training officers and fellow rookies, Tim Bradford and Lucy Chen, Angela Lopez, Wesley Evers, Nyla Harper, Sergeant Wade Grey, the Mid-Wilshire station, key cases and arcs across the seasons, the relationships and partnerships, and the themes of second chances, mentorship and duty that define the series.
