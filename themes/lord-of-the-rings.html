Lord of The Rings Trivia Questions

149 questions across multiple rounds

A fantasy epic about fellowship, war, and the struggle against Sauron in Middle-earth. It covers key characters, places, battles, and legendary quest moments.

This quiz is built for fans who remember both the major moments and the smaller details from Lord of The Rings, including character dynamics, memorable scenes, recurring jokes, quotes, and storylines across the series.

Marathon Mode is best for a longer run with bigger rounds, while Challenge Mode works better for shorter 10-question sessions. Episode Mode is also available for selected themes where questions can be played episode by episode.

Sample Questions

  • Which tower does the Eye of Sauron sit atop?
  • What creature pulls Grond, the battering ram?
  • Which actress portrayed Arwen, daughter of Elrond?
