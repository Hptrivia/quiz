Control trivia works because Remedy's supernatural shooter drops Jesse Faden into the Federal Bureau of Control's ever-changing headquarters. Fans can be tested on the Oldest House, the Hiss, the Objects of Power and the game's eerie lore.

A strong Control quiz can include Jesse Faden and her search for her brother Dylan, the Federal Bureau of Control and the Oldest House, the Hiss corruption and the Board, the Service Weapon and its forms, Objects of Power and Altered Items, the Astral Plane and the game's live-action segments, and the developer and publisher behind it. It works because it rewards players who paid attention to both the combat and the world-building.
