Ozark trivia works because the series follows Marty Byrde laundering cartel money while his family is pulled into an ever-tightening web of crime. Fans can be tested on the Byrdes, their enemies and the deals that keep them alive.

A strong Ozark quiz can include Marty and Wendy Byrde and their children, the cartel and its enforcers, the Langmore and Snell families, the money-laundering schemes and the casino, the local players and FBI investigators, the betrayals and body count, and the choices that transform the family across the seasons. It works because it rewards fans who tracked every scheme and shifting alliance.
