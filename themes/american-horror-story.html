American Horror Story trivia works because each season is a self-contained nightmare, from Murder House and Asylum to Coven and Hotel, tied together by a recurring cast. Fans can be tested on the characters, the settings and the scares across the anthology.

A strong American Horror Story quiz can include the Harmon family and the Murder House, Briarcliff and the horrors of Asylum, the witches of Coven, the Hotel Cortez and Freak Show, the recurring actors who play new roles each season, the ghosts, monsters and villains, and the connections that link the seasons together. It works because it rewards fans who follow both the standalone stories and the shared universe.
