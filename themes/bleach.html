Bleach trivia works because the series combines a large cast of characters with a structured power system built around Zanpakuto, Bankai transformations, and faction-based conflicts that evolve across long story arcs.

The theme works because it balances character recognition, power system knowledge, and long-running narrative progression across multiple story phases.
