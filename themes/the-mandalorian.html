The Mandalorian trivia works because it brings a western, bounty-hunter feel to the Star Wars galaxy with a beloved central duo and rich world-building. Fans can be tested on Din Djarin, Grogu, and the journey across the outer rim.

A strong The Mandalorian quiz can include Din Djarin and the Way of the Mandalore, Grogu and his Force abilities, the Razor Crest, Greef Karga and the Bounty Hunters' Guild, the Armorer and the Covert, beskar and Mandalorian culture, Cara Dune, Kuiil and IG-11, Moff Gideon and the darksaber, Boba Fett and Bo-Katan, the planets and quarry of each chapter, and the themes of loyalty, found family and creed that define the series.
