Widows Bay trivia works because the series follows Ruth, a widow whose everyday seaside routine conceals secrets that ripple through the town. Fans can be tested on the characters, the setting and the show's slow-burn mysteries.

A strong Widows Bay quiz can include Ruth and her late husband, Tom and the other townsfolk, the seaside community and its routines, the elderly neighbours Ruth looks after, the secrets and revelations that drive the story, the key relationships and turning points, and the finale's memorable moments. It works because it rewards fans who pay attention to a character-driven story full of quiet detail.
