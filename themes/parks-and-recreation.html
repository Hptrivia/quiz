Parks and Recreation trivia works because the series combines local-government absurdity with very memorable characters, wholesome relationships, and repeated jokes tied to Pawnee itself. It is one of those sitcoms where the setting feels almost as important as the cast.

A strong Parks and Recreation quiz can include Leslie, Ben, Ron, April, Andy, Tom, Ann, Donna, Jerry, the Parks Department, city council drama, Harvest Festival, local businesses, campaign stories, weddings, and Pawnee-specific traditions or nonsense. The theme works because it can balance character affection, small-town absurdity, workplace stories, and the repeated details that make fans feel at home in that world.
