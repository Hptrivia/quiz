Arcane trivia works especially well because the series leaves very strong visual and emotional memory through its characters, class conflict, technology, and tragic relationships. Even viewers who watched it once often remember specific scenes, designs, and turning points.

A strong Arcane quiz can include Vi, Jinx, Caitlyn, Jayce, Viktor, Silco, Mel, Hextech, Shimmer, Piltover, Zaun, family bonds, betrayals, and the political tension between progress and exploitation.
