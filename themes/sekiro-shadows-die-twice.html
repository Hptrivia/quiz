  • What skill allows you to counter thrust attacks by stepping on the blade? Mikiri Counter
  • What is the name of the NPC who lets you practice combat at the temple? Hanbei the Undying
  • What is the name of the protagonist's primary katana? Kusabimaru
  • How many endings does Sekiro have? 4
  • What happens to your XP and Sen when you die permanently? You lose half of it
  • What is the primary material used to upgrade prosthetic tools to their final forms? Lapis Lazuli
  • What is the title of the game's final main story boss (in most endings)? Isshin, the Sword Saint
  • Which button is used to perform a 'Deathblow' when an enemy's posture is broken? R1/RB
  • What status effect causes rapid health loss and is represented by a purple bar? Terror
  • What do you receive from the Sculptor to unlock new skill trees? Esoteric Texts

Sekiro trivia works because the game is built around precision combat, timing-based deflection, and high-stakes boss encounters that reward mastery rather than progression grinding. Every encounter feels memorable due to its difficulty and design clarity.

A strong Sekiro quiz can include Wolf, Kuro, Genichiro Ashina, Isshin the Sword Saint, Lady Butterfly, and major factions such as the Ashina clan. It also covers mechanics like posture breaks, resurrection, stealth assassinations, prosthetic tools, and combat arts. The theme works because it tightly blends gameplay systems with narrative progression, where each boss and area is mechanically distinct and heavily tied to the player’s learning curve.
