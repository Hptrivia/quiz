Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice Trivia Questions

100 questions across multiple rounds

A game trivia theme focused on Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, covering its combat system, bosses, stealth mechanics, world design, and the Sengoku-era inspired setting.

Sekiro trivia works because the game is built around precision combat, timing-based deflection, and high-stakes boss encounters that reward mastery rather than progression grinding. Every encounter feels memorable due to its difficulty and design clarity.

A strong Sekiro quiz can include Wolf, Kuro, Genichiro Ashina, Isshin the Sword Saint, Lady Butterfly, and major factions such as the Ashina clan. It also covers mechanics like posture breaks, resurrection, stealth assassinations, prosthetic tools, and combat arts. The theme works because it tightly blends gameplay systems with narrative progression, where each boss and area is mechanically distinct and heavily tied to the player’s learning curve.

Marathon Mode is best for a longer run with bigger rounds, while Challenge Mode works better for shorter 10-question sessions.
