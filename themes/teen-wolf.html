Teen Wolf trivia works because the series turns a werewolf bite into high-school drama, pack loyalty and escalating supernatural threats in Beacon Hills. Fans can be tested on the characters, the creatures and the storylines across its seasons.

A strong Teen Wolf quiz can include Scott McCall and his transformation, Stiles, Derek Hale and the pack, Allison Argent and the hunters, Lydia the banshee, Kira, Malia and Peter Hale, the creatures from the Kanima to the Nogitsune, Beacon Hills and the Nemeton, the alphas and the hunts, and the lacrosse, romances and mysteries that define the show. It works because it rewards fans who ran with the pack.
