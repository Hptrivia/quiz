Off Campus trivia works because it blends college hockey, slow-burn romance and sharp banter into an addictive new-adult story. Fans can be tested on the Briar University crew, the central relationships, and the show's standout moments.

A strong Off Campus quiz can include Garrett Graham and his hockey career, Hannah Wells and her music ambitions, the fake-relationship and tutoring deal, the Briar University team and rivals, Logan, Dean, Tucker and the housemates, Justin and Malone's, the games and parties, the family backstories, and the romance, friendship and college-life themes that define the series.
