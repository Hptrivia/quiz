  • What is the name of the film producer Michael works with in the story? Solomon Richards
  • Which of these is NOT a radio station in GTA V? K-DST
  • Which company developed Grand Theft Auto V? Rockstar Games
  • What is the name of the video game series that Michael’s son Jimmy is obsessed with? Righteous Slaughter
  • What is the name of the cult Michael can join through a series of tasks and donations? Epsilon Program

  • Which character is a corrupt FIB agent who frequently pressures the protagonists throughout the story? Steve Haines

GTA V trivia works because the game is full of recognisable missions, characters, neighbourhoods, businesses, radio culture, and chaos-driven gameplay moments that players remember long after they finish the story. It has enough scale to support both casual and deeper fan questions.

A strong GTA V quiz can include Michael, Franklin, Trevor, Lester, major heists, the FIB, gangs, Los Santos and Blaine County locations, businesses, vehicle types, weapons, hobbies, strangers and freaks, and specific story missions or set pieces. The theme stays strong because it can move between narrative detail, map knowledge, gameplay systems, side content, and the exaggerated tone of the world, rather than relying only on a few famous scenes.
