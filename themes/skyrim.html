Skyrim trivia works because the open-world RPG drops the Dragonborn into a vast province of dragons, Daedra and civil war. Fans can be tested on the quests, the holds, the shouts and the endless lore of Tamriel's north.

A strong Skyrim quiz can include the Dragonborn and the return of Alduin, the nine holds and their cities, the main quest and the Greybeards, the guild questlines from the Companions to the Thieves Guild and Dark Brotherhood, the Daedric Princes and their artifacts, dragon shouts and words of power, the civil war between the Imperials and Stormcloaks, and the creatures and characters of the province. It works because it rewards players who explored every corner of Skyrim.
