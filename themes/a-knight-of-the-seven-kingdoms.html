A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms works well as trivia because it trades the huge sprawl of later Westeros stories for more personal stakes, clearer relationships, and specific events built around Ser Duncan the Tall and Egg. That makes the details easier to anchor while still keeping the world rich.

A strong A Knight of the Seven Kingdoms quiz can draw from Dunk, Egg, Ashford, tourneys, hedge-knight life, noble houses, lineage, vows, honour, and the quieter politics of Westeros before the events of Game of Thrones. The theme has a different strength from the bigger franchise entries because it can focus more tightly on named characters, local conflicts, and the social structure of the realm without needing giant war-scale plotlines.
