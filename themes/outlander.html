  • What famous, devastating battle do Jamie and Claire try to prevent? Battle of Culloden
  • What is the name of Claire's husband in the 20th century? Frank
  • Where does the Artemis run aground after the storm in Season 3? Georgia
  • What university post has Frank accepted at the start of the episode? Oxford
  • In what month and year does Claire tell Jamie the Jacobites will be wiped out on Culloden Moor? April 1746
  • What is the name of Sir Marcus MacRannoch's estate where Claire and the others take shelter? Eldridge House
  • What is Mrs. Fitzgibbons's role at Castle Leoch? Housekeeper
  • What year is Claire living in when the story begins? 1945
  • What is the first name of Brianna's love interest? Roger
  • What is the name of the attending physician who delivers Claire's baby? Dr. Thorne

Outlander trivia works because it combines time travel, sweeping historical settings and an epic romance across centuries, giving questions both adventure and emotional depth. Fans of Diana Gabaldon's novels and the series can be tested on the journeys, the history, and the Claire–Jamie relationship.

A strong Outlander quiz can include Claire Randall/Fraser, Jamie Fraser, Frank Randall, the standing stones at Craigh na Dun, Castle Leoch and Clan MacKenzie, the Jacobite rising and Culloden, Black Jack Randall, Brianna and Roger, the move to colonial America and Fraser's Ridge, key locations across Scotland, France and the New World, and the time-travel romance based on Diana Gabaldon's novels.

If you enjoy Outlander trivia, also try Daredevil and Rick and Morty on Trivia Gauntlet.
