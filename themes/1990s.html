  • Which 1994 Disney movie featured a genie voiced by Robin Williams? (released 1992) Aladdin
  • Which 1997 Radiohead album is widely hailed as a landmark of the decade? OK Computer
  • Which rapper was fatally shot in Las Vegas in September 1996? Tupac Shakur
  • What is the name of the fast blue hedgehog created by Sega? Sonic the Hedgehog
  • Which Disney animated film features the song 'Hakuna Matata'? The Lion King
  • Which 1995 film featured the catchphrase 'To infinity and beyond'? Toy Story
  • Which 1996 Celine Dion power ballad later became the 'Titanic' theme? (theme released 1997) My Heart Will Go On
  • Which Sega console launched in North America in 1996 to compete with the PlayStation? Sega Saturn
  • Which website, founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos, started by selling books online? Amazon
  • Which 1996 movie paired Michael Jordan with the Looney Tunes? Space Jam

1990s trivia works because the decade packed in unforgettable music, movies, TV, games and tech that millions still remember vividly. It blends nostalgia with broad pop-culture and historical knowledge that almost everyone can engage with.

A strong 1990s quiz can include grunge and boy bands, hip-hop's golden era, blockbuster films and indie cinema, sitcoms and cartoons, the rise of the internet and dial-up, game consoles and arcade hits, toys and crazes, fashion trends, sports moments, and the major political and cultural events that shaped the decade. It works because it can move between pop culture, technology and history while tapping into strong nostalgia.
