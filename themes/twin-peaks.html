  • Who does Cooper address all of his tape recordings to? Diane
  • What is the name of the town's local newspaper? Twin Peaks Gazette
  • Which band performs at the Roadhouse at the end of Episode 1 The Return? Chromatics
  • What does Dougie vomit before being transported to the Red Room? Creamed peas
  • What is the benevolent counterpart to the Black Lodge? White Lodge
  • What is the name of the high school principal who announces Laura's death? George Wolchezk
  • What object does Margaret Lanterman constantly carry? A log
  • What historical figure does Benjamin Horne obsessively impersonate after his breakdown? Robert E. Lee
  • Where was Teresa Banks living at the time of her death? Fat Trout Trailer Park
  • Which Renault brother tended bar at One-Eyed Jacks and dealt drugs to local teens? Jacques Renault

Twin Peaks trivia works because the series blends a central murder mystery with surreal, dreamlike storytelling and an unforgettable ensemble of small-town characters, giving questions a mix of plot, atmosphere and iconic imagery. Its cult status means fans remember even the strangest details.

A strong Twin Peaks quiz can include the murder of Laura Palmer, Special Agent Dale Cooper and his coffee and cherry pie, the Double R Diner, the Black Lodge and the Red Room, BOB and the Log Lady, Audrey Horne and the Horne family, Sheriff Harry S. Truman and the local lawmen, the owls and the famous 'damn fine coffee' lines, and the surreal mythology that runs through the series and its revival.
