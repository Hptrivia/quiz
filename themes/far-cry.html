Sample Questions

Tap an answer to reveal it.

  • Which doctor grows drugs and cares for Daisy in Far Cry 3? Dr. Alec Earnhardt
  • Which of these is NOT a real Far Cry title? Far Cry Legends
  • What are animal companions like Boomer called in Far Cry 5? Fangs for Hire
  • Who is Pagan Min's ruthless associate and enforcer in Far Cry 4? Yuma Lau
  • Who runs the fighting arena that the player must escape in Far Cry 4? Noore Najjar
  • In the original Far Cry, what are the mutated enemies created by experiments called? Trigens
  • Far Cry New Dawn is a direct sequel to which game? Far Cry 5
  • What are the backpack-mounted special weapons in Far Cry 6 called? Supremos
  • In the Shangri-La sequences of Far Cry 4, the player controls which legendary warrior? Kalinag
  • What is the name of the cyborg commando hero in Far Cry 3: Blood Dragon? Rex Power Colt

Far Cry trivia works because each game strands the player in a beautiful, hostile open world run by a charismatic tyrant. Fans can be tested on the villains, the regions and the chaos across Far Cry 3, 4, 5 and beyond.

A strong Far Cry quiz can include Vaas Montenegro and the island of Rook, Pagan Min and Kyrat, Joseph Seed and Hope County, Anton Castillo and Yara, the player characters from Jason Brody to Dani Rojas, the outposts, wingsuits and takedowns, the animals and hunting, the weapons and vehicles, and the co-op and companions that define the series. It works because it rewards players who liberated every outpost.

If you enjoy Far Cry trivia, also try Demon Souls and Baldurs Gate 3 on Trivia Gauntlet.
