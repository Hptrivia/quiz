Uncharted trivia works because the series follows Nathan Drake chasing lost treasures across cinematic, globe-trotting adventures. Fans can be tested on the locations, the allies and the historical mysteries that drive each hunt.

A strong Uncharted quiz can include Nathan Drake and his mentor Sully, allies and rivals like Elena, Chloe and his brother Sam, the treasures hunted from El Dorado to Libertalia, the exotic locations and set-piece chapters, the climbing, gunplay and puzzle-solving, the villains chasing the same prizes, and the story that unfolds across the series. It works because it rewards fans who followed Drake's adventures to the end.
