World Football Trivia Questions

208 questions across multiple rounds

A broad football trivia theme covering the global game through international stars, clubs, tournaments, records, rivalries, and famous moments from different leagues and competitions.

World football trivia works because the sport gives you endless material across World Cups, Champions League nights, league giants, legendary players, and unforgettable goals or finals. It is broad enough to feel huge, but familiar enough that most players have a way into it.

A strong World Football quiz can include Pelé, Maradona, Messi, Ronaldo, Zidane, World Cup winners, European champions, famous clubs, Ballon d'Or winners, derby rivalries, tournament history, records, stadiums, and iconic goals or controversies. The category works because it can move between club and international football, balancing old legends with modern stars and giving rounds enough global variety to stay fresh.

Marathon Mode is best for a longer run with bigger rounds, while Challenge Mode works better for shorter 10-question sessions.

Sample Questions

  • Which player received the first-ever red card in a FIFA World Cup tournament (1970)?
  • Which country’s football team is known as the ‘Three Lions’?
  • Which player scored in the final for AC Milan in both the 1989 and 1990 European Cups?
  • Which club is nicknamed 'The Toffees'?
  • Which club did Platini play for when he won three consecutive Ballon d’Or awards (1983–85)?
  • Which goalkeeper won the inaugural Yashin Award (Golden Glove) at the 2018 World Cup?
  • Which competition is contested by national teams in Europe every four years?
  • Which country eliminated Brazil in the 2014 FIFA World Cup semi-final?
  • Which team did Bayern Munich defeat in the 2020 UEFA Champions League final?
  • Which national football team is nicknamed 'La Roja'?
