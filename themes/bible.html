Bible Trivia Questions

111 questions across multiple rounds

A scripture trivia theme covering major books, key figures, important stories, teachings, symbols, and memorable moments from the Bible. It is designed around broad familiarity with biblical content rather than narrow theological debate.

Bible trivia works well because it covers a huge range of recognisable material, from Genesis and Exodus to the Gospels, parables, kings, prophets, apostles, and major miracles. It gives casual players plenty of entry points while still leaving room for harder questions on people, places, sequences, and specific events.

A strong Bible quiz can draw from creation, Noah, Abraham, Moses, David, Solomon, the prophets, the life of Jesus, the disciples, and the early church, alongside commandments, books of the Bible, famous verses, symbols, and locations. That range helps the theme feel fuller than a narrow religious round, because questions can shift between narrative memory, character recognition, teaching, chronology, and general knowledge that many players have picked up over time.

Marathon Mode is best for a longer run with bigger rounds, while Challenge Mode works better for shorter 10-question sessions.

Sample Questions

  • What did God set in the sky as a sign of His covenant after the flood (Genesis 9)?
  • Who’s wife was turned into a pillar of salt?
  • Who interpreted dreams for Pharaoh in Egypt?
  • What did Jesus feed 5,000 people with?
  • Who was the judge known for great strength and a vow involving his hair?
  • Who was David’s son who became king after him?
  • What was Jesus’ first recorded miracle in John’s Gospel?
  • Which book contains the story of a man who suffered greatly yet remained faithful?
  • Who was thrown into a den of lions?
  • What did God create on the first day?
