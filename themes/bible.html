Sample Questions

Tap an answer to reveal it.

  • How many disciples did Jesus have? 12
  • Which book focuses heavily on wisdom sayings, many attributed to Solomon? Proverbs
  • Who was the Roman governor who presided over Jesus’ trial in the Gospels? Pilate
  • Who’s wife was turned into a pillar of salt? Lot
  • What is the name of the holy day commemorating Jesus’ resurrection? Easter
  • Which prophet book focuses on a locust plague as a picture of the Day of the Lord and calls for repentance? Joel
  • What is the first book of the Bible? Genesis
  • What is the name of the garden where Jesus prayed before His arrest? Gethsemane
  • Which New Testament letter urges believers to present their bodies as a living sacrifice and not be conformed to this world but be transformed by the renewing of their minds? Romans
  • Which apostle had a vision of unclean animals and was led to the Gentiles? Peter

Bible trivia works well because it covers a huge range of recognisable material, from Genesis and Exodus to the Gospels, parables, kings, prophets, apostles, and major miracles. It gives casual players plenty of entry points while still leaving room for harder questions on people, places, sequences, and specific events.

A strong Bible quiz can draw from creation, Noah, Abraham, Moses, David, Solomon, the prophets, the life of Jesus, the disciples, and the early church, alongside commandments, books of the Bible, famous verses, symbols, and locations. That range helps the theme feel fuller than a narrow religious round, because questions can shift between narrative memory, character recognition, teaching, chronology, and general knowledge that many players have picked up over time.
