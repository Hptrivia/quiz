  • The nuclear war in Fallout is known as what? The Great War
  • What group does Reg lead inside the vault that Betty dissolves? Inbreeding Support Group
  • What sub-floor does Robert House say the MacLean family cryo chambers are on? Sub-floor seven
  • Which chem does Lucy become dependent on during Season 2? Buffout
  • Where does Thaddeus say he was born in? The Boneyard
  • In Season 2, what is the name of the faction captain who greets the Ghoul in the hills? Captain Rodriguez
  • What is the name of Mr. House's casino headquarters? Lucky 38
  • What critical piece of Vault 33 infrastructure is revealed to be destroyed? Water Chip
  • Where did Moldaver hold her pre-war anti-Vault-Tec meetings? Hollywood Forever Cemetery
  • Where does Moldaver have her base of operations? Griffith Observatory

Fallout trivia works because the series brings the games' retro-futuristic wasteland to life with memorable characters, Vaults, factions and dark humour. Fans can be tested on the show's story, its world-building and its links to the wider Fallout universe.

A strong Fallout quiz can include Lucy, the Ghoul, Maximus and the Brotherhood of Steel, Vault 33 and the Vault-Tec system, the NCR and the wasteland factions, the bombs and the world before the war, the retro-futuristic technology, Nuka-Cola and Pip-Boys, and the connections between the show and the games. It works because it blends a rich post-apocalyptic setting with characters, lore and mystery.
