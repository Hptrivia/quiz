Fallout trivia works because the series brings the games' retro-futuristic wasteland to life with memorable characters, Vaults, factions and dark humour. Fans can be tested on the show's story, its world-building and its links to the wider Fallout universe.

A strong Fallout quiz can include Lucy, the Ghoul, Maximus and the Brotherhood of Steel, Vault 33 and the Vault-Tec system, the NCR and the wasteland factions, the bombs and the world before the war, the retro-futuristic technology, Nuka-Cola and Pip-Boys, and the connections between the show and the games. It works because it blends a rich post-apocalyptic setting with characters, lore and mystery.
