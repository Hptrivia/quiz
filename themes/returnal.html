Sample Questions

Tap an answer to reveal it.

  • What signal is Selene searching for? The White Shadow
  • How many Echoing Keys must Selene find to open Hyperion's vault? Two
  • Which weapon fires blobs of acid that pool on the ground? Rotgland Lobber
  • Which company published Returnal? Sony Interactive Entertainment
  • Which resource is used to activate a Reconstructor to respawn after death? Ether
  • What item is retrieved from an alien corpse to operate the xeno-tech teleporter? Hermetic Transmitter
  • Who developed Returnal? Housemarque
  • What is the name of the level 1 adrenaline buff that enlarges the reload target area? Enhanced Overload
  • The three biomes of Act 1 are Overgrown Ruins, Crimson Wastes, and which third? Derelict Citadel
  • A stronger version of the Titanops appears in the Tower of Sisyphus. What is it called? Proto-Titanops

Returnal trivia works because the roguelike shooter strands astronaut Selene on Atropos, dying and restarting through a shifting alien world. Fans can be tested on the biomes, the weapons and the story's unsettling mysteries.

A strong Returnal quiz can include Selene and her crashed ship Helios, the planet Atropos and its biomes from the Overgrown Ruins onward, the time loop and the cycle of death and rebirth, the alien weapons, artifacts and parasites, the enemies and bosses, the house sequences and the story's psychological twists, and the developer behind it. It works because it rewards players who pieced together the loop's fragmented narrative.

If you enjoy Returnal trivia, also try Red Dead Redemption 2 and The Last of Us on Trivia Gauntlet.
