Suits trivia works because the show mixes courtroom strategy with office politics, personal loyalty, status games, and very memorable character chemistry. Fans tend to remember both the major legal conflicts and the repeated habits, lines, and rivalries that define the firm.

A strong Suits quiz can include Harvey, Mike, Jessica, Louis, Donna, Rachel, Pearson Specter and its later forms, mergers, rival firms, promotions, betrayals, mock trials, negotiations, and the many episodes built around Mike's hidden lack of a law degree. The theme works because it can move between legal drama, workplace hierarchy, relationships, and the specific character traits that made the show's dialogue and power struggles so recognisable.
