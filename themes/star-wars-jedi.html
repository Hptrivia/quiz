Star Wars Jedi trivia works because Fallen Order and Survivor follow Cal Kestis, a Padawan surviving the Empire's purge. Fans can be tested on the planets, the crew, the lightsaber combat and the story across both games.

A strong Star Wars Jedi quiz can include Cal Kestis and his droid BD-1, the crew of the Mantis, the planets explored from Bogano and Zeffo to Koboh, the Inquisitors and Imperial threats, the lightsaber forms and Force powers, the exploration, puzzles and mini bosses, and the story that runs from Fallen Order into Survivor. It works because it rewards fans who followed Cal's journey across the galaxy.
