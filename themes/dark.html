Dark trivia works because the German Netflix series weaves time travel, missing children and interlinked families into one of TV's most intricate puzzles. Fans can be tested on Winden, the timelines and the show's mind-bending mysteries.

A strong Dark quiz can include Jonas, Martha, Mikkel and Ulrich, the Kahnwald, Nielsen, Doppler and Tiedemann families, the Winden nuclear power plant and the cave passages, the 33-year time cycles and the 1888/1953/1986/2019/2052 timelines, the bootstrap paradoxes and Adam and Eva, the apocalypse, and the show's recurring symbols and quotes. It works because it rewards close attention to a famously complex, interlocking story.
