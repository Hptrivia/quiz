Andor trivia works because it tells a grounded, political Star Wars story about how ordinary people are pushed into rebellion, with rich characters and slow-burn tension. Fans can be tested on Cassian's journey, the Empire's machinery, and the early spark of the Rebellion.

A strong Andor quiz can include Cassian Andor and his origins on Kenari and Ferrix, Luthen Rael and his rebel network, Mon Mothma's political balancing act, Dedra Meero and the ISB, Syril Karn and Pre-Mor security, the Aldhani heist, the Narkina 5 prison, Bix, Brasso, Maarva and the people of Ferrix, Saw Gerrera, and the themes of sacrifice, surveillance and resistance that define the series.
