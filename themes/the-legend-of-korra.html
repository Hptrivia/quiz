The Legend of Korra trivia works because the series follows a bold new Avatar navigating a modern, industrial world and four books of escalating threats. Fans can be tested on Korra, her allies and the villains of Republic City.

A strong The Legend of Korra quiz can include Korra's mastery of the four elements and the Avatar State, Team Avatar including Mako, Bolin and Asami, the setting of Republic City and its politics, the antagonists across the four books from Amon to Kuvira, the spirits and the spirit world, bending and pro-bending, and the connections to Avatar: The Last Airbender. It works because it rewards fans who followed Korra's growth across every book.
