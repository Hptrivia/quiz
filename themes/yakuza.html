Yakuza trivia works because the series blends a serious crime saga with over-the-top brawls, substories and the neon streets of Kamurocho. Fans can be tested on the characters, the story and the world across Kiryu's saga and Like a Dragon.

A strong Yakuza quiz can include Kazuma Kiryu the Dragon of Dojima, Goro Majima, Ichiban Kasuga and the shift to turn-based combat, the Tojo Clan and rival families, Kamurocho and Sotenbori, the substories and minigames, Haruka and Kiryu's found family, the heat actions and fighting styles, and the twists across the mainline games and Kiwami remakes. It works because it rewards fans who walked every alley of Kamurocho.
