  • What drug does Dr. Faber use to keep Ben sedated? Secobarbital
  • During which war was Ben Reilly serving when he got his powers? World War I
  • What nickname did the soldiers give the young veteran who claimed to have lied about his age to enlist? Freckles

Spider-Noir trivia works because the series reimagines the Spider as a weary 1930s private eye in a shadowy, noir New York. Fans can be tested on Ben Reilly, the period setting and the show's gritty mysteries.

A strong Spider-Noir quiz can include Ben Reilly as The Spider, his life as a 1930s photographer and private investigator, his late fiancée and his secretary, the years he spent retired before being drawn back, the noir New York setting and its villains, the central mysteries and confrontations, and the show's distinctive black-and-white period style. It works because it blends a familiar hero with a fresh, hard-boiled detective story.
