Grey's Anatomy trivia works because the show combines memorable medical cases with intense romances, friendships, rivalries, and major tragedies that fans remember for years. It gives you both hospital drama and personal drama in the same category.

A strong Grey's Anatomy quiz can include Meredith, Derek, Cristina, Alex, Bailey, Richard, Callie, Arizona, Izzie, the hospital's name changes, big surgeries, plane crashes, weddings, breakups, mentorships, and the professional and personal choices that shape each era of the show. The theme works because it can balance medicine, relationships, character exits, and the many emotional turning points long-time viewers still remember clearly.
