Wednesday trivia works because the show mixes mystery, supernatural elements, school drama, and gothic humour around a very distinct main character. The setting and tone make even small details memorable.

A strong Wednesday quiz can include Wednesday Addams, Enid Sinclair, Nevermore Academy, principal Weems, Thing, psychic visions, outcast groups, Hyde monster storyline, local mysteries, rival families, and key investigation plotlines. The theme works because it combines detective-style storytelling, supernatural abilities, character dynamics, and a visually distinctive world that is easy to recognise.
