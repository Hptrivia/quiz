God of War trivia works because the newer games combine mythological spectacle with very personal storytelling, giving players memorable realms, named gear, dramatic boss fights, and strong family tension all at once. That makes the subject much richer than a simple mythology-themed action game.

A strong God of War quiz can include Kratos, Atreus, Mimir, Freya, Baldur, Thor, Odin, the Leviathan Axe, Blades of Chaos, realms such as Midgard and Asgard, major bosses, side quests, companions, and the shifting father-son dynamic that drives the story. The theme stays strong because it can move between combat systems, Norse mythology, exploration, emotional development, and the specific scenes that made the newer games stand out.
