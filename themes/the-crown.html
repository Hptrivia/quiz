The Crown trivia works because it dramatises real twentieth-century history through the British monarchy, blending political events with private royal drama across several decades. Fans can be tested on the historical milestones, the royal figures, and the series' interpretation of events.

A strong The Crown quiz can include Queen Elizabeth II, Prince Philip, Winston Churchill and successive prime ministers, Princess Margaret, the Queen Mother, the abdication legacy of Edward VIII, key historical events such as the King's death, the coronation, the Suez crisis and Aberfan, royal residences such as Buckingham Palace and Balmoral, the changing cast across seasons, and the tension between duty and personal life that defines the show.
