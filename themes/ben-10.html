Ben 10 trivia works because the franchise combines a simple hook — a kid with a watch that turns him into aliens — with a deep roster of transformations, villains and continuity across multiple series. Fans can be tested on individual alien forms, recurring enemies, and the lore that spans the original run and its sequels.

A strong Ben 10 quiz can include Ben Tennyson and the Omnitrix, Gwen and Grandpa Max, the Plumbers, alien forms like Heatblast, Four Arms, XLR8, Diamondhead and Upgrade, villains such as Vilgax, Kevin Levin and the DNAliens, the summer road trip premise, the Ultimatrix and Omnitrix variations across series, and key transformations and abilities that fans remember.
