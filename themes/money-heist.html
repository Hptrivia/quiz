111 questions across multiple rounds

A Spanish crime series about a team of robbers carrying out elaborate heists under the Professor’s plan. It blends tension, strategy, and emotional character drama.

This quiz is built for fans who remember both the major moments and the smaller details from Money Heist, including character dynamics, memorable scenes, recurring jokes, quotes, and storylines across the series.

Marathon Mode is best for a longer run with bigger rounds, while Challenge Mode works better for shorter 10-question sessions. Episode Mode is also available for selected themes where questions can be played episode by episode.
