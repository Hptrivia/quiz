Curb Your Enthusiasm trivia works because Larry David's improvised social disasters, feuds and faux pas are endlessly quotable. Fans can be tested on the characters, the episodes and the cringe comedy that defines the show.

A strong Curb Your Enthusiasm quiz can include Larry David, Cheryl, Jeff and Susie Greene, Leon, Richard Lewis and the celebrity guests, the social rules and faux pas Larry breaks, iconic episodes and storylines, catchphrases like 'pretty, pretty good', the improvised style, the Seinfeld reunion arc, and the running feuds that drive each season. It works because it blends a famous creator with sharp, cringe-inducing observational comedy.
