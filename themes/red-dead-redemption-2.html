Red Dead Redemption 2 trivia works especially well because the game leaves players with strong memory of gang members, camps, towns, missions, random encounters, hunting, and the slow collapse of the outlaw life. It is one of those games where both the world and the relationships stick.

A strong Red Dead Redemption 2 quiz can draw from Arthur Morgan, Dutch, John Marston, Micah, Hosea, Sadie, gang camps, Saint Denis, Valentine, Rhodes, weapons, horses, honour, robberies, stranger missions, and major chapters in the story. The theme has real depth because it combines emotional character-driven memory with open-world systems, map knowledge, side content, and the smaller details players remember from spending long stretches inside its world.
