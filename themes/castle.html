Sample Questions

Tap an answer to reveal it.

  • In 'Cuffed', Castle and Beckett wake up handcuffed together and trapped with what animal? A tiger
  • Who plays Richard Castle? Nathan Fillion
  • In the Season 1 finale, what family does Jimmy Moran's evidence threaten to bring down? The Spolanos
  • What is the name of the first murder victim shown on screen in the pilot episode? Alison Tisdale
  • What graphic novel did Castle reveal at the precinct at the start of the Season 3 finale? A Deadly Storm
  • What weapon did the sniper use to shoot Beckett at Montgomery's funeral? Mark 11 modified sniper rifle
  • What film double feature had Castle planned to watch alone after Alexis's graduation? The Killer and Hard-Boiled
  • Castle's second ex-wife Gina also works as his what? Publisher
  • In the Season 2 premiere, which pair did Castle first compare himself and Beckett to when calling them a good team? Starsky and Hutch
  • What is the name of the Castle novel whose launch party opens the pilot episode? Storm Fall

Castle trivia works because it pairs a witty bestselling novelist with a sharp homicide detective, mixing playful banter and romance with weekly murder mysteries. Fans can be tested on the cases, the supporting team, and the Castle–Beckett relationship.

A strong Castle quiz can include Richard Castle and his novels, Detective Kate Beckett, the 12th Precinct, Detectives Esposito and Ryan, Captain Montgomery and Captain Gates, Castle's daughter Alexis and mother Martha, medical examiner Lanie Parish, the mystery of Beckett's mother's murder, recurring villains, Castle's Nikki Heat books, and the slow-burn partnership and romance that drives the series.
