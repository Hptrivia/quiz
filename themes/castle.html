Castle trivia works because it pairs a witty bestselling novelist with a sharp homicide detective, mixing playful banter and romance with weekly murder mysteries. Fans can be tested on the cases, the supporting team, and the Castle–Beckett relationship.

A strong Castle quiz can include Richard Castle and his novels, Detective Kate Beckett, the 12th Precinct, Detectives Esposito and Ryan, Captain Montgomery and Captain Gates, Castle's daughter Alexis and mother Martha, medical examiner Lanie Parish, the mystery of Beckett's mother's murder, recurring villains, Castle's Nikki Heat books, and the slow-burn partnership and romance that drives the series.

If you enjoy Castle trivia, also try The Vampire Diaries and Dexter on Trivia Gauntlet.
