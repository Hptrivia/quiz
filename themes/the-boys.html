The Boys trivia works because the show flips the normal superhero formula into something cynical, brutal, and highly memorable. Viewers tend to remember its powers, shocks, corporate manipulation, and character betrayals very clearly.

A strong The Boys quiz can include Butcher, Hughie, Homelander, Starlight, Queen Maeve, The Deep, A-Train, Vought, Compound V, The Seven, major scandals, media control, and the violent confrontations that define each season. The theme works because it can move between superhero identity, corporate branding, politics, gore, and the many moments where public image collides with private corruption.
