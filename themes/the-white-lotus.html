The White Lotus trivia works because each season builds a tight, character-driven world inside a single resort setting, with a small ensemble whose relationships and agendas become increasingly layered. The anthology format means fans have two very distinct casts and locations to draw from.

A strong The White Lotus quiz can include the Hawaii and Sicily settings, characters like Tanya, Armond, Shane, Rachel, Belinda, and the Season 2 ensemble of Ethan, Harper, Daphne and Cameron, the resort staff dynamics, recurring themes of wealth and privilege, the deaths that bookend each season, family dysfunction, infidelity, and the specific moments that became widely talked about after each episode.
