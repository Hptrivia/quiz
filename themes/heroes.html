Heroes trivia works because the series follows ordinary people around the globe who discover they have superhuman abilities, their lives colliding as they try to stop a catastrophe. Fans can be tested on the powers, the characters and the interwoven storylines across its volumes.

A strong Heroes quiz can include Claire Bennet, Hiro Nakamura, Peter and Nathan Petrelli, Mohinder Suresh, Matt Parkman and the villain Sylar, the powers from regeneration to space-time manipulation, the Company and Primatech, the 'save the cheerleader, save the world' mission, the eclipse and the Godsend symbol, and the twists across seasons like Generations, Villains, Fugitives and Redemption. It works because it rewards fans who followed the show's sprawling web of heroes and villains.
