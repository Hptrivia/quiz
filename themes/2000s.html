2000s trivia works because the decade combined a music and movie boom with the rise of the internet, social media and modern gadgets. It blends nostalgia with broad pop-culture and tech knowledge that resonates strongly with younger players.

A strong 2000s quiz can include pop and emo, reality TV and teen dramas, blockbuster franchises, early social media and viral internet culture, MP3 players and flip phones, game consoles and online gaming, fashion trends, celebrity moments, and the major news and cultural events of the decade. It works because it can move between pop culture, technology and history while tapping into millennial nostalgia.
