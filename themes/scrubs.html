Scrubs trivia works because the show mixes fast-paced workplace comedy with surreal daydreams and genuine emotional moments, giving questions a wide range from running gags to character relationships. Its medical setting and tight ensemble make it instantly recognisable to fans.

A strong Scrubs quiz can include J.D. (John Dorian) and his narration and fantasies, Turk and their bromance, Elliot Reid, Carla Espinosa, Dr. Cox and his rants, Dr. Kelso, the Janitor and his feud with J.D., Sacred Heart Hospital, recurring gags and nicknames, the friendship and relationship arcs, and the blend of comedy and dramatic moments the series is known for.
