Glee trivia works because the musical dramedy mixes big covers, high-school rivalries and the New Directions' road to Nationals. Fans can be tested on the characters, the songs and the storylines across the seasons.

A strong Glee quiz can include Rachel Berry and Finn Hudson, Kurt, Blaine and the Warblers, Santana, Brittany, Quinn and Puck, Mr. Schuester and the ever-scheming Sue Sylvester, the New Directions and rival clubs, the show choir competitions from Sectionals to Nationals, the guest stars and tribute episodes, and the standout musical numbers that defined the show. It works because it rewards fans who knew every solo and slushie.
