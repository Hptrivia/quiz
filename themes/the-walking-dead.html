The Walking Dead

A zombie apocalypse series about survival, shifting communities, and moral collapse in a brutal world. It mixes undead horror with constant human conflict.

This quiz is built for fans who remember both the major moments and the smaller details from The Walking Dead, including character dynamics, memorable scenes, recurring jokes, quotes, and storylines across the series.

Marathon Mode is best for a longer run with bigger rounds, while Challenge Mode works better for shorter 10-question sessions. Episode Mode is also available for selected themes where questions can be played episode by episode.
