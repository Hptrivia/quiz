Borderlands trivia works because the series pairs frantic looter-shooter gunplay with a cast of unforgettable characters, alien worlds and endless weapons. Fans can be tested on the Vault Hunters, the villains and the lore that runs from the first game through Borderlands 4.

A strong Borderlands quiz can include the Vault Hunters and their action skills, iconic characters like Handsome Jack, Lilith, Claptrap and the Calypso Twins, the planets and settings from Pandora onward, the guns, manufacturers and legendary weapons, the Vaults and the creatures guarding them, the major bosses and story missions of Borderlands 3 and 4, and the series' signature comedy. It works because it rewards fans who know both the loot and the lore.
