Sample Questions

Tap an answer to reveal it.

  • Which actress won Best Actress at the AMAA for her role in *Shattered*? Rita Dominic
  • Who directed the biopic film 'Ayinla'? Tunde Kelani
  • Which veteran directed *Magun*, a film blending culture and suspense? Tunde Kelani
  • Which veteran actress played Mama G in many classic Nollywood films? Patience Ozokwor
  • Which veteran actor is famous for playing strict father figures, royalty and speaking Igbo proverbs? Pete Edochie
  • Which actress starred in the film *The Bling Lagosians* as the matriarch? Elvina Ibru
  • Which actor is known for his 'bad boy' roles in Nollywood? Jim Iyke
  • Which 2016 thriller features Adesua Etomi playing a character who turns to crime for survival? The Set Up
  • Which filmmaker directed the visually intense short film *Hello, Rain*? C.J. Obasi
  • Which film also called Araromire tells a story of love and sacrifice between two friends and a woman? The Figurine

Nollywood trivia works because the industry has produced a huge number of recognisable stars, films, and story patterns that audiences remember well, from family drama and romance to comedy and crime. That gives it a stronger identity than a vague general movie round.

A strong Nollywood quiz can draw from famous actors and actresses, directors, hit films, classic titles, newer productions, genre trends, industry milestones, and the wider influence of Nigerian cinema across Africa and beyond. The theme works because it can move between celebrity recognition, film knowledge, cultural context, and the evolution of one of the world’s most productive film industries.
