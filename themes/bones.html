Bones trivia works because the series blends forensic procedural cases with a long-running partnership and romance between a brilliant scientist and an FBI agent, giving questions both case detail and character depth. Fans can be tested on the science, the squints, and the Brennan–Booth relationship.

A strong Bones quiz can include Dr. Temperance "Bones" Brennan, Special Agent Seeley Booth, the Jeffersonian Institute, Angela Montenegro, Jack Hodgins, Camille Saroyan, Zack Addy, Lance Sweets, the rotating squinterns, forensic anthropology and bone analysis, memorable killers such as the Gormogon and Christopher Pelant, Brennan's novels and family, and the slow-burn partnership that becomes the heart of the show.
