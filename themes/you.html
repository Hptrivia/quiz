You trivia works because the series is built entirely around Joe Goldberg's warped perspective — his rationalizations, fixations and escalating crimes give questions a very specific psychological texture that fans find compelling. Each season introduces a new setting and love interest, expanding the cast and stakes considerably.

A strong You quiz can cover Joe's love interests across all seasons including Guinevere Beck, Love Quinn and Marienne, Joe's obsessive behaviour patterns and methods, the basement cage, Paco, Ethan and supporting characters, Love's own dark side, the Madre Linda setting, Joe's new identities, Rhys Montrose in season four, the London arc, and the recurring theme of Joe rebranding himself while repeating the same cycles.
