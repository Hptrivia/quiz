Mr. Robot trivia works because the series follows Elliot Alderson, a hacker recruited by fsociety to bring down E Corp while his grip on reality slips. Fans can be tested on the hacks, the characters and the show's mind-bending twists.

A strong Mr. Robot quiz can include Elliot Alderson and his work at Allsafe, the fsociety collective and its members, the plan to take down E Corp, the mysterious Mr. Robot himself, Darlene, Angela, Tyrell and the other key players, the real-world hacking and cybersecurity details, and the reveals that reshape the story. It works because it rewards fans who paid close attention to an unreliable narrator.
