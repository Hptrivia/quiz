Anime trivia works because it spans a huge variety of series with strong visual identity, emotional storytelling, and distinctive power systems.

A strong anime quiz can include Naruto, One Piece, Dragon Ball, Attack on Titan, Jujutsu Kaisen, and Demon Slayer, alongside characters, transformations, abilities, arcs, and major battles. The category is effective because anime often builds structured worlds with clear rules, emotional stakes, and highly memorable moments that translate well into trivia questions.
