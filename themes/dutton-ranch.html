Dutton Ranch trivia works because the Yellowstone continuation moves Beth, Rip and Carter to Texas for a fresh fight over land, cattle and family. Fans can be tested on the new ranch, the characters and the show's hard-edged Western drama.

A strong Dutton Ranch quiz can include Beth and Rip, Carter, the move to Texas and the new ranch, the rodeo and characters like Hoyt Boone, the cattle business and the clashes over land, the bunkhouse cowboys and ranch life, the ties back to Yellowstone and the Dutton legacy, and the major storylines and confrontations. It works because it carries Yellowstone's intensity into a new setting fans are eager to learn.
