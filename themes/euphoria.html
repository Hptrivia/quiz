Euphoria trivia works because it portrays modern teenage life through an intense, stylised lens of addiction, love and self-discovery, with bold visuals and complex characters. Fans can be tested on the storylines, the relationships, and the show's distinctive moments.

A strong Euphoria quiz can include Rue Bennett and her narration and addiction, Jules Vaughn, Nate Jacobs, Maddy Perez, Cassie Howard, Kat Hernandez, Fezco and Ashtray, Lexi Howard, Cal Jacobs, the high school and party settings, key relationships and betrayals, Lexi's play, and the themes of addiction, identity and trauma that define the series.
