Batman: Arkham trivia works because the games drop the Dark Knight into a freeform Gotham packed with gadgets, predator takedowns and Batman's greatest villains. Fans can be tested on the story, the combat and the world across Asylum, City, Origins and Knight.

A strong Batman: Arkham quiz can include Bruce Wayne and the Batman, the Joker and his schemes across the series, villains from Scarecrow and Bane to the Riddler and Two-Face, the gadgets and the Batmobile, the islands and districts of Gotham, the freeflow combat and predator sections, the side missions and Riddler trophies, and the twists that run from Arkham Asylum to Arkham Knight. It works because it rewards players who cleaned up every corner of Gotham.
