  • What is the in-game currency? Groschen
  • What color are sage blossoms according to Bozhena's description? Purple
  • Which quest immediately follows Easy Riders in Kingdom Come Deliverance 2? Fortuna
  • What is the name of the maid Henry can speak to in the baths at Lord Pisek's estate? Maid Zuzana
  • From what perspective are the games primarily played? First-person
  • What primarily drives Henry throughout the story? Revenge for his murdered parents
  • Who is Henry's sweetheart in Skalitz, a tavern girl who hands him five Saviour Schnapps? Bianca
  • Which German knight, serving King Sigismund, is blamed for the Skalitz massacre and becomes a target of Henry's revenge? Markvart von Aulitz
  • What is the first main quest in Kingdom Come Deliverance 2? Last Rites
  • In Deliverance II, which monastery does Henry explore to stop the spread of a deadly illness? Sedletz Monastery

Kingdom Come: Deliverance trivia works because the series drops Henry into a realistic medieval Bohemia with no magic, just history, combat and survival. Fans can be tested on the story, the systems and the world across both games.

A strong Kingdom Come: Deliverance quiz can include Henry and his journey from blacksmith's son to fighter, the historical setting of 15th-century Bohemia, the towns, castles and factions, the realistic combat, skills and reputation systems, the quests and characters that shape the story, and the additions and starting classes of Deliverance 2. It works because it rewards fans who appreciate the game's commitment to historical detail.
