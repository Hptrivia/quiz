The Originals trivia works because it follows the oldest and most dangerous vampire family as they reclaim New Orleans, blending family loyalty, supernatural politics and betrayal. Fans of The Vampire Diaries universe can be tested on the Mikaelsons, the factions, and the lore.

A strong The Originals quiz can include Klaus, Elijah and Rebekah Mikaelson, the rest of the Original family, Marcel Gerard, Davina Claire, Hayley Marshall and the hybrid baby Hope, the witches of the French Quarter, the werewolf and vampire factions, New Orleans settings, the hybrid curse and the white oak, recurring villains, and the family-above-all theme of "always and forever" that defines the show.
