Criminal Minds trivia works because the show pairs a rotating gallery of memorable unsubs with a tight-knit BAU team whose dynamics evolve over many seasons, giving questions both case-of-the-week variety and long-running character depth. The focus on profiling and psychology gives the trivia a distinctive angle compared with other crime dramas.

A strong Criminal Minds quiz can include the Behavioral Analysis Unit and its FBI role, Aaron Hotchner, Derek Morgan, Spencer Reid, Penelope Garcia, David Rossi, Jennifer Jareau and Emily Prentiss, Gideon's early leadership, recurring unsubs like the Reaper and the Replicator, the team's profiling methods and jet, opening and closing quotes, and major arcs across the long run of the series.
