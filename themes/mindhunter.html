Sample Questions

Tap an answer to reveal it.

  • What is Holden Ford's primary job role at the start of the series? Hostage Negotiator
  • What city's police department handles the case of the murdered woman in a junkyard (Season 1)? Altoona
  • Who is the local Atlanta activist mother who leads the Committee related to the Child Murders? Camille
  • What was the name of the victim found in the Altoona junkyard? Beverly Jean Shaw
  • Which interview tape goes missing in Season 1, threatening the unit's reputation? The Richard Speck interview
  • What is the name of the blunt, veteran local detective in the Fairfield Police Department who interacts with Bill and Holden in the pilot episode? Detective Frank McGraw
  • What does the BSU stand for in the series? Behavioral Science Unit
  • On which streaming service did Mindhunter originally air? Netflix
  • In Season 2, which bridge are the police and FBI monitoring when they finally hear the splash that leads to catching Wayne Williams? James Jackson Parkway Bridge
  • What item does Richard Speck infamously throw into an electric fan during his interview? A bird

Mindhunter trivia works because the series draws directly from real FBI history and real serial killers, giving questions a grounded edge that fans of true crime and psychological drama both respond to. The blend of procedural detail and character psychology makes it rich territory for a quiz.

A strong Mindhunter quiz can include Holden Ford and Bill Tench, the Behavioral Science Unit, serial killer interviews with figures like Ed Kemper and Charles Manson, criminal profiling methods, Quantico, Wendy Carr's research role, Holden's personal unravelling, the BTK storyline, and the tension between academic theory and fieldwork that drives the series.
