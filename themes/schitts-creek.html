Schitt's Creek trivia works because the series builds a very specific world around a small group of characters, each with a distinct voice, running jokes, and a clear personal arc. The contrast between the Rose family's wealthy past and their new small-town life gives questions plenty of varied ground to cover.

A strong Schitt's Creek quiz can include the four Rose family members and their roles, town residents like Roland, Jocelyn, Stevie and Twyla, the Rosebud Motel, Rose Apothecary, Moira's acting career and wig collection, David and Patrick's relationship, Alexis's many pre-Creek adventures, Johnny's business plans, Sunrise Bay, Café Tropical, town elections, and signature moments like the fold-in-the-cheese scene and Patrick's Simply the Best proposal.
