Breaking Bad trivia works because the show is packed with highly memorable character shifts, tense scenes, lies, betrayals, and symbols that viewers tend to carry with them long after finishing it. It is one of the clearest examples of a series where story progression itself becomes trivia gold.

A strong Breaking Bad quiz can include Walter, Jesse, Skyler, Hank, Saul, Gus, Mike, the DEA, Los Pollos Hermanos, the RV, blue meth, rival dealers, cartel conflict, lab operations, and the major decisions that turn Walt from teacher to kingpin. The theme works because it can balance character psychology, criminal structure, family fallout, visual symbols, and the specific episode-by-episode details that made the series so gripping.
