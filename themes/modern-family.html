Modern Family trivia works because the series has a very clear ensemble structure, with each branch of the family bringing its own habits, tensions, and comic patterns. That makes both the big life events and the small recurring jokes easy for fans to remember.

A strong Modern Family quiz can include Jay, Gloria, Manny, Claire, Phil, Haley, Alex, Luke, Mitchell, Cameron, Lily, the family homes, jobs, holidays, school and parenting stories, and the many habits or lines associated with each character. The theme works because it can balance family relationships, household-specific humour, emotional moments, and the repeated details that make the show feel familiar and rewatchable.
