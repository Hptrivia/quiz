Elden Ring trivia works especially well because the game mixes huge exploration with memorable bosses, named regions, complicated questlines, and lore fragments that players piece together over time. That gives it both mainstream recognition and deep fan-level detail.

A strong Elden Ring quiz can pull from Limgrave, Liurnia, Caelid, Altus Plateau, Leyndell, the Haligtree, the Tarnished, the demigods, the Greater Will, major NPC quests, endings, weapons, spells, ashes, summons, and bosses such as Margit, Radahn, Morgott, Malenia, and Maliketh. The theme works because it can shift between combat, geography, factions, item knowledge, and hidden story detail, which makes it richer than a simple list of bosses and locations.
