The Office trivia works especially well because the show is full of recognisable characters, repeated habits, small workplace humiliations, and emotional relationship beats that fans remember after countless rewatches. Even tiny details from the office setting tend to stick.

A strong The Office quiz can include Michael, Dwight, Jim, Pam, Angela, Andy, Kevin, Creed, Dunder Mifflin, Scranton branch chaos, pranks, parties, romances, HR issues, and the many small routines or disasters that define office life. The theme works because it can balance broad sitcom recognition with very specific scene memory, which is exactly why the show remains such a strong rewatch and trivia subject.
