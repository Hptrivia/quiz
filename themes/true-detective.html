Sample Questions

Tap an answer to reveal it.

  • In Season 1, what members of the new task force does Major Quesada introduce to Rust and Marty in the office? Daughtry, Bertrand, Dufrene
  • Which actress plays Detective Liz Danvers in Season 4? Jodie Foster
  • What was the name of the young woman at the trailer-park brothel whom Marty believed was underage? Beth
  • In Season 1, what program did Rust Cohle work for during his four years undercover, referred to by its acronym? HIDTA
  • What does Rust say a person's sentience is like, from a fourth-dimensional perspective? Carts on a track
  • What church group do Rust and Marty track down while investigating who Dora Lange had been attending services with? Friends of Christ Revival
  • What year was the Childress and Son Maintenance business license first issued, according to records Marty finds? 1978
  • In Season 1, what medication does the nurse call for when Kelly Reider goes into distress at the psych ward? Haldol
  • What is the name of Reggie Ledoux's known associate that Rust and Marty track down for information on Ledoux's whereabouts? Tyrone Weems
  • What body part belonging to the murdered activist is found at the Tsalal research station in Season 4? A tongue

True Detective trivia works because each season is a self-contained story with a distinct cast, setting and tone, making it rich territory for questions that reward either broad series knowledge or deep dive into a single season. The contrast between seasons — from Rust Cohle's nihilism to the Night Country's supernatural edge — gives fans a lot to work with.

A strong True Detective quiz can include Rust Cohle and Marty Hart in Louisiana, the Yellow King mythology, Frank Semyon and the Vinci corruption arc, Wayne Hays and the Purcell case in Arkansas, Liz Danvers and Evangeline Navarro in Alaska, the spiral symbol across seasons, the anthology format itself, key crimes and suspects, and the philosophical monologues that became the series' signature.
