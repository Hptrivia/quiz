True Detective trivia works because each season is a self-contained story with a distinct cast, setting and tone, making it rich territory for questions that reward either broad series knowledge or deep dive into a single season. The contrast between seasons — from Rust Cohle's nihilism to the Night Country's supernatural edge — gives fans a lot to work with.

A strong True Detective quiz can include Rust Cohle and Marty Hart in Louisiana, the Yellow King mythology, Frank Semyon and the Vinci corruption arc, Wayne Hays and the Purcell case in Arkansas, Liz Danvers and Evangeline Navarro in Alaska, the spiral symbol across seasons, the anthology format itself, key crimes and suspects, and the philosophical monologues that became the series' signature.
