  • Which of these Pokémon evolves when exposed to a Water Stone? Eevee
  • What is the name of the tower where you encounter Ghost Pokémon in Lavender Town? Pokémon Tower
  • Which Pokémon must hold a Razor Claw and level up at night to evolve? Sneasel
  • Which Pokémon is the mascot of the franchise? Pikachu
  • What are the first two Pokémon games released worldwide? Red and Blue
  • Which Pokémon was the first Mythical Pokémon revealed? Mew
  • Which Mythical Pokémon was first distributed with the Azure Flute event that was never officially released? Arceus
  • In Pokémon Sun and Moon, which Pokémon serves as the mascot for Team Skull’s Guzma? Golisopod
  • Which Pokémon is known as the 'Legendary Sea Guardian'? Lugia

Pokémon trivia works because the franchise is full of instantly recognisable creatures, type matchups, regions, gyms, evolutions, and named characters that many players have known since childhood. It is broad enough to work for casual fans but detailed enough to reward deeper franchise memory.

A strong Pokémon quiz can include Kanto, Johto, Hoenn, Sinnoh, starters, Gym Leaders, Elite Four members, type advantages, evolution methods, legendary and mythical Pokémon, Poké Balls, moves, held items, villain teams, and anime crossover knowledge. The theme stays rich because it can test players on both broad franchise familiarity and very specific game systems, species knowledge, and region-by-region memory.
