How It Works

TriviaGauntlet lets you browse quiz categories, pick a theme, and start answering questions right away. You can search for a specific show, game, sport, or topic, or explore by category.

Each quiz presents multiple-choice questions and keeps track of your score as you play. At the end of the quiz, you’ll see your final result and can jump straight into another round or try a different theme.

More game modes, more quiz formats, and more features are planned over time. The platform is built to keep expanding while staying simple and fast to use.
