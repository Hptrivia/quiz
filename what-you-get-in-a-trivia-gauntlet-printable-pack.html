What You Get in a Trivia Gauntlet Printable Pack

A Trivia Gauntlet printable pack is designed to give players more than just a basic quiz file.

Each pack includes three files:

  • a JSON upload file
  • a PDF version
  • a DOCX version

Together, these files make the pack useful both for playing online in the upload version of Trivia Gauntlet and for offline or printable trivia use.

1. The JSON Upload File

The first file is the main upload file.

This is a machine-friendly quiz file that contains the questions, answer options, and correct answers in a format that works with the Trivia Gauntlet upload system. It is not really meant to be read casually on its own. Its main purpose is to let you load the theme into the upload version of Trivia Gauntlet and play it in a cleaner, more flexible format.

Once you download the file, you can save it on your phone, laptop, or another device, go to the upload page, and load the pack directly into the game.

After uploading the pack, you can play it across multiple modes.

2. Marathon Mode

In Marathon Mode, you can play through the full question set in one run.

If the pack includes 100 questions, you can play all 100 in a single stretch. You answer each question in order, move through the full set, and see your final score at the end.

This is the best option for players who want the most traditional long-form trivia experience.

3. Challenge Mode

Challenge Mode breaks the pack into shorter 10-question rounds.

That makes it easier to play in smaller sessions instead of committing to the full set at once. It also works well if you want to jump in quickly, test yourself, or share a shorter round with someone else.

It is a lighter and more flexible way to play the same question set.

4. Survival Mode

Survival Mode turns the pack into a more gamified experience.

In this mode, one wrong answer ends the run. The difference with a purchased pack is that you get access to unlimited lifelines, including:

  • 50-50
  • Call a Friend

That makes the mode more fun and more forgiving than the free version, where lifelines are limited. It also makes it useful for casual play, retries, and group sessions.

5. Episode Mode

For selected themes, the pack also works with Episode Mode.

This mode is more focused and scene-based. After you answer, it tells you whether you were right or wrong and shows the correct answer. For some themes, it also includes an extra explanation or description to give more context.

That makes Episode Mode especially good for fans who enjoy more detailed and more guided trivia.

6. Versus Mode

The pack also supports Versus Mode.

This is designed for two players using the same device. Player One answers, then Player Two answers, and both scores are tracked so you can see who wins the round.

It is one of the easiest ways to turn the same pack into a quick head-to-head quiz session.

7. The PDF Version

The printable PDF version is for offline play.

This is useful if you want to:

  • print the quiz
  • use it at a party
  • run a game night
  • play with friends away from the screen

The PDF gives you a cleaner printable format, while the separate answer sheet makes it easier to host or score.

8. The DOCX Version

The DOCX version gives you a more editable copy of the pack.

That is useful if you want to:

  • make small changes
  • remove questions
  • adjust formatting
  • customize the pack for your own event

9. Fully Editable and Customizable

One of the best parts of the pack is that it is not locked down.

Because the files are editable, you can:

  • delete questions you do not want
  • add your own questions
  • change options
  • customize the set for your own use

That makes the pack more flexible than a normal fixed quiz download.

10. Good for Trivia Nights and Group Play

The point of the pack is not just to give you extra files.

It is to give you different ways to use the same theme:

  • upload and play online
  • play in a cleaner format
  • use more flexible modes
  • print and host offline
  • customize the pack for your own group

That is what makes it more than just a basic download.

Final Thought

A Trivia Gauntlet printable pack is built to be flexible.

You are not just buying one file. You are getting a theme in multiple usable formats, with options for online play, offline printing, group play, and customization.

At €2.50 per theme, it is designed to give players more ways to enjoy a quiz than the standard free version alone.
