About TriviaGauntlet

TriviaGauntlet is a quiz platform built for fans of TV series, games, sports, movies, books, and general knowledge. The goal is simple: make trivia fast, fun, and easy to jump into without unnecessary friction.

You can browse by category, search for a specific theme, and start playing within seconds. New quizzes and new topics are added regularly, with more features and game modes planned over time.

Whether you want to test yourself, challenge friends, or just play through your favorite fandoms, TriviaGauntlet is designed to give you a quick and enjoyable quiz experience.
